How To Play

Minesweeper

Left click or touch to open a cell. Right click or hold to flag a cell. You can use the select on the side panel to change the left click or touch default action.

Click anywhere to begin. You are guaranteed to have at least a 3x3 of non mines on your first click. The number in a cell shows how many mines are in the surrounding 8 cells. Once all non mine cells have been opened, you have won. If you click a mine, then you lose.

Save Games

Every game you play will be saved unless you disable the checkbox. From the main menus, you can start playing any save game or delete them. If you have a lot, you can turn on multiselect mode to delete multiple at a time. Click the edit icon to change the name of the save game.
